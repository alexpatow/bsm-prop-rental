Pricing and Payment Policies

Pricing

  • Minimum rental $50.00
  • Items are not individually priced, please contact Scott Effertz
  • We want to work with you, so we can quote prices on individual items or for show packages

Payment

We accept:
  • Credit Card
  • Personal, school, company, or organizational Checks
  • Cash

We do not accept purchase orders.

Deposits

We require some type of deposit commensurate with the value of the item(s). This deposit should take the form of a check which will be returned or shredded when the rental items are returned in good condition and in a timely manner.

Additional Policies

  • Items are rented in “As is..” condition
  • Items should be returned in the same or better condition as they were received
